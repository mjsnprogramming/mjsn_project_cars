1911-1920

The years 1911-1920 were a time of dynamic development of brands such as Mercedes, Cadillac, Chevrolet, Ford and Peugeot.
The fastest car of that time was the Austro-Daimler Prinz Heinrich with a speed of 136 kph.

1886-1910

In 1886, Carl Benz built the first vehicle in Mannheim, the Patentwagen No. 1, which can be fully called a car.
For over 100 years since that event, cars have changed beyond recognition.
The fastest car of that time was the Mercedes 60HP Simplex with a speed of 118 kph.
